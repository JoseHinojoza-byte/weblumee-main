About Weblumee

Built in Chicago. Built for Small Businesses.

Weblumee is a freelance web development studio based in the Chicagoland area, focused on helping small businesses build a serious online presence without paying agency prices. Every website is hand-coded from scratch — no WordPress, no Wix, no drag-and-drop shortcuts.

That means your site is faster, cleaner, and more SEO-friendly than anything a page builder can produce. Whether you need your first website or a replacement for something that's been holding you back, the process is simple, transparent, and built around your goals.

One developer, one point of contact, and zero surprises — just a website that actually works for your business.
